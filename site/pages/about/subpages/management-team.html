Owner and CEO:
Phil Bucket

Phil is a fitness guru. He is an outdoor enthusiast and has backpacked all over the world. However, on an expedition in the Tabet, Phil became lost and was on his own for 18 months. During this time, he developed his exercise program. His program is called Heavy Bucket and has become one of the most popular fitness regiments among semiprofessional Pickel Ball players.

Director of Personnel:
Sunshine Dasiy

Sunshine Dasiy became the Director of Personnel in 2018 after escaping from a fitness cult in Nevada. She is in charge of all patrons and employees at Fit 4You. She taught herself Astrophysics and is a leading Yogini after spending the covid quarantine watching yoga on PBS.

Chief Financial Officer:
Rupert Balthazar Hamish Hugo III

Rupert Balthazar Hamish Hugo III received his accounting degree from Cambridge University at the age of 13. He made a fortune laundering money for the Mob in the '70s and '80s. After the arrests of significant mafia members, Rupert played the stock market, becoming the wealthiest man in the world in 1997. Unfortunately, he made risky investments in BlockBuster, Radioshack, Sears, Myspace, XFL, and DeLorean Motor Co. He lost nearly all his fortune and was forced to work at Fit 4 You due to his criminal background.
